**G-Index Hub Kazakhstan** is an institutional platform for business assessment and monitoring, designed to develop:

- Women-led entrepreneurship  
- Inclusive supply chains  
- Sustainable economic growth in Central Asia and CICA countries

# 3. Problem Statement

Kazakhstan:

- 180,000 SMEs  
- 46% of GDP  
- <5% integrated into supply chains  

**Gap: 41 percentage points**

### Core Issues

- Lack of verification → no trust  
- Lack of capability → no access  

> 70–90% of SMEs are excluded not due to quality, but due to lack of verification systems

# 6. DOT — Execution Layer

**DOT (Digital Operator of Trade)**

### Core Principle

### Execution Flow

2. Milestones defined  
3. Execution occurs  
4. Evidence captured  
5. Verification  
6. DOT decision
